Digital Transformation in Bussiness

Digitization (the conversion), Digitalization (the process) and the digital transformation (the effect) therefore accelerate and illuminate the already existing and ongoing horizontal and global processes of change in society.

Digital Bussiness Transformation is organizational change through the use of digital technologies and bussiness model to improve performance. Digital Bussiness Transformation is not a state of being, it is a journey anh this journey is guided by three questions: Why ? What ? and How ?

Why transform ?

There are three main reasons for this transformation. They are: Consumers, New Competitors and Emerging Technologies

  • Consumers now are better informed than ever before. They are actively searching for enhanced services, high product qualities at lower prices. They can visit an commerce store, search for information about the products ang producers then buy them online
  • Transformation also comes from new competitors who are better at meeting the consumers
    The obvious example in Vietnam is the tough competition of traditional taxi businesses with Grab, although Grab appears much later
  • Emerging Technologies enable new capabilities. These new technologies could provide points of competive differentitation if adopted first or internalized and integrated in some new ways

What ?

Google can see each company has seven distinct categories, any of which could be transformed digitally. They are: bussiness model, structure, people, the processes, IT, capability – offerings and engagement model
However, it is difficult to achieve digital bussiness transformation benefits by making a single change. So, the last question is How to transform ?

How to transform ?

Among three questions, this is the hardest to answer
Knowing what to do and how to do it are two very different challenges
There is no one-size-fits-all approach to successful digital bussiness transformation. However, it’s essential that organizations need develop a fundamental capability that we call digital bussiness transformation agility. This capability is made up of three components: hyper-awareness, informed decision making fast execution
